Good Afternoon, Ms. Silver Soul

1. Write answers of the following questions :
  1. Define buffer with examples.
  2. Classify extra- and intra-cellular electrolytes with examples.
  3. What are dentrificing agents? Give examples.
  4. What are antacids? Give examples.
  5. What is achlorhydria? Give its treatment.
  6. Define saline cathartic; write its examples.
  7. What are antimicrobial agents? Give its examples.
  8. Define emetics with examples.
  9. Define isotopes and write its examples.
  10. Define antidotes with examples.
2. Write answer of any two of the following :
  1. What are antacids? Write their ideal properties. Write the preparation, assay and uses of sodium bicarbonate.
  2. What are antimicrobial agents? Write its classification. Write a note on preparation and assay of boric acid.
  3. What are various sources of impurities? Write in detail about limit test for sulphate.
3. Write answers of the following (any seven) :
  1. Give a detailed account of the history of pharmacopoeia.
  2. What are buffers? Write importance of buffer solutions in pharmacy.
  3. Write a note on electrolytes used in replacement therapy.
  4. Discuss the role of fluoride in dental caries.
  5. Give the method of preparation and uses of aluminium hydroxide gel.
  6. Define saline cathartics. Write its mechanism of action.
  7. Describe various mechanisms of action of inorganic antimicrobial agents.
  8. What are haematinics? Write the preparation and assay of ferrous sulphate.
  9. What are expectorants? Give method of assay of ammonium chloride.

  2. Define acids & bases according to various concepts.

  6. Write the preparation & uses of chlorinated lime & boric acid.
  7. What are expectorants? Give example & mechanism of action.
  8. Explain diagnostic & therapeutic applications of radioisotopes.
